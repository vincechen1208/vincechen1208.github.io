CS 184: Computer Graphics and Imaging, Spring 2018

Final Project: 3D Fluid Simulation

Vincent Chen, Sheng-Yu Wang, Haw-Wei Lin

Updated Plan

Instead of building PBD from scratch, we've decided to use the CUDA sample (particles) and build real time 3D liquid simulation. We've implemented a constraint solver based on the algorithms described in [Macklin et al. 2013] to enforce incompressibility. To make the simulation more realistic, we've also resolved tensile instability and accounted for vorticity confinement and viscosity. Our next step would be to implement shaders to render different liquid surfaces. Although we have changed our plans, we are on track to finish everything we originally planned to do, including finishing surface rendering, which was part of our aspirations in our proposal.
